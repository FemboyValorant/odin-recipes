Mashed Potatoes

Credit to comfynb on tumblr

Description

"Here's a mashed potato recipe from my homophobic mother that I swore to never share that would pair perfectly!" -comfynb

(Good for 6 servings)

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs of red potatoes
  • 1 cup of butter (or 2 sticks)
  • 1 cup of cream cheese (equal to 1 pack)
  • Chives
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash the potatoes and put in a big pot
  2. Boil until soft
  3. Drain water from pot
  4. Mash potatoes until chunky mix
  5. Add all other ingredients and keep mashing
