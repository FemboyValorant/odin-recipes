Chili

Description

Just some simple tomato-based chili made with five ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • 15 oz of tomato sauce
  • 1 can of kidney beans, drained
  • 1 can of chili beans, undrained
  • 2 tablespoons of chili power, or to taste
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, around 6 minutes
  3. Stir in all beans, and tomato sauce. Boil and stir in chili powder
  4. Reduce heat to a simmer and cook, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 15 minutes
  5. Season to taste with salt and pepper
